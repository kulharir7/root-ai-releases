The Fundamental Difference

ChatGPT is a conversation tool. You ask it questions, it generates answers. It's brilliant at writing, explaining, brainstorming, and analyzing text. But it can't do anything on your computer.

Korvus is an action tool. It uses the same AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, or local models) but adds the ability to actually interact with your system. It can read your files, run your code, control your browser, and automate your workflow.

ChatGPT is like texting a smart friend for advice. Korvus is like having that friend sit at your computer and do the work for you.

When to Use ChatGPT

  • Writing essays, emails, or creative content
  • Brainstorming ideas
  • Explaining complex topics
  • Analyzing text you paste into the chat
  • Quick Q&A on any topic

When to Use Korvus

  • Organizing files on your computer
  • Running and debugging code
  • Automating browser tasks (scraping, form filling)
  • Deploying applications
  • Managing your calendar, email, and notifications
  • Controlling smart home devices
  • Running scheduled background tasks
  • Anything that requires action, not just words

The Privacy Factor

This is a big one. When you use ChatGPT, every message goes to OpenAI's servers. They see your prompts, your data, everything. For casual use, that's fine. For sensitive work data, personal files, or business secrets? That's a risk.

Korvus gives you a choice:

  • Use Ollama → Everything stays on your machine. Zero data leaves. Full privacy.
  • Use OpenAI/Anthropic → Only your queries go to the cloud. Your files stay local.

You can even use different models for different tasks. Use Ollama for personal file management (100% private) and GPT-4 for complex reasoning tasks.

The Cost Factor

ChatGPT Plus: $20/month for GPT-4 access. Limited tool use. No computer control.

Korvus Free: ₹0 forever. Full file/terminal access. Ollama integration (completely free AI). Basic plugins.

Korvus Pro: ₹499/month (~$6). Everything — voice, browser, phone, all channels, all plugins.

Korvus Pro costs less than one-third of ChatGPT Plus and does ten times more.

Can They Work Together?

Yes! Korvus can use OpenAI's GPT-4 as its AI brain. So you get ChatGPT's intelligence plus Korvus's ability to act. Best of both worlds.

Or use Claude, Gemini, Mistral, or any other model. Korvus is model-agnostic — you pick the brain, it provides the hands.

The Verdict

ChatGPT and Korvus aren't competitors — they're different categories. ChatGPT is a chatbot. Korvus is an agent.

If you just need to chat with AI? ChatGPT is great. If you need AI to actually do things on your computer? That's Korvus.
